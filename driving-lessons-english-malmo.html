How the Swedish Driving License Process Works

Getting your Swedish B-category driving license involves several steps:

1

Apply for körkortstillstånd

Apply for your learner's permit (körkortstillstånd) from Transportstyrelsen online. You need a valid ID.

2

Risk Training (Risk 1 & 2)

Complete mandatory risk education: Risk 1 (theory about alcohol, drugs, fatigue) and Risk 2 (practical skid track training).

3

Driving Lessons

70-minute lessons in Malmö traffic with Andre. We'll build your skills step by step.

4

Theory Test

Pass the Swedish theory test at Trafikverket. Available in multiple languages including English.

5

Driving Test (Uppkörning)

When you're ready, take your practical driving test at Trafikverket. We'll prepare you thoroughly.
