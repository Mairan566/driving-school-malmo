What is the motorcycle theory test?

To get a motorcycle licence in Sweden (A1, A2 or A), you must pass a theory test at Trafikverket. The test covers traffic rules, road signs, risk awareness and motorcycle-specific topics like balance, positioning and visibility. The test consists of 65 questions, and you need at least 52 correct answers to pass.

What the theory test covers

The Trafikverket motorcycle theory test includes questions on these key areas:

Traffic rules & signs

Swedish traffic regulations, road signs, right-of-way rules and speed limits.

Risk awareness

Hazard perception, effects of alcohol and drugs, fatigue, and how to handle dangerous situations.

Road behaviour

Lane positioning, safe following distance, overtaking, riding in different weather and road conditions.

MC-specific topics

Balance and steering, braking techniques, protective equipment, passenger riding and motorcycle maintenance.
